    A typical day in my current life involves me waking up at 7:50AM, eating breakfast, and rushing to school. After school I sometimes have Programming Club, sometimes Math club, and other times have hockey, often getting home at 7PM due to these activities. Then, I usually take a nap because I’m tired from the day and also because I have a bad sleep schedule. Due to the (in my opinion completely unnecessary) onslaught of physics homework, I often spend up to an hour getting that finished; sometimes there’s also homework from other classes, which I have to spend even more time doing. After finishing homework, I often spend copious amounts of time surfing the web doing activities one does not need to worry about. I sleep on average at around 1:30AM.
